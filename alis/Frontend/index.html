The Challenge

  • Fraud Detection

    Identifying unusual biometric/demographic update patterns manually across 3.7 million records is statistically impossible without AI.

  • Resource Allocation

    Reactive kit deployment leads to long queues in high-demand zones while machinery sits idle in others.

The ALIS Solution

  • Predictive Intelligence

    SARIMA + XGBoost ensemble models forecast enrollment center demand 30 days in advance with 87% verifiable accuracy.

  • Automated Risk Scoring

    Real-time evaluation of every pincode using a multi-factor risk engine (Bio, Demo, Mobile linkage gaps).
